About

Data Science Summit organized by Department of Artificial Intelligence and Centre of Excellence in Data Science will take place at G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur, India on 12th & 13th November 2019. The summit will bring together industry, academia and research organizations to share and exchange knowledge and opportunities available in the area of Data Science. This Summit will bring the experts from Abroad, government and semi government Agencies, researchers from IITs, NITs, IIITs and from various research labs in India and abroad at this common platform, where they will contribute their experience during panel discussion, power talk & case studies The startup showcase will be another exciting feature wherein startup will share their experiences and challenges.
